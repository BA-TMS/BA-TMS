## How to Sign Up

This guide explains how a **Company Owner** can sign up for TMS

### Prerequisites

- A valid email address
- Company details (Company Name, Address, Phone Number, Docket Number)

### Steps to Sign Up

1. **Access the Sign-Up Page**

- Navigate to the sign-up page by clicking "Sign Up" on the login page or at [http://localhost:3000/signup](http://localhost:3000/signup)

2. **Enter Company Details**

- Fill in the required fields:

  - Company Name
  - Company Telephone
  - Company Address
  - Docket Number and Type

  ![Company Sign Up Page](/assets/screenshots/companyDetailsSignup.png)

3. **Enter User Details**

- Fill in the required fields for you as a user:

  - First Name
  - Last Name
  - Email
  - Personal Telephone
  - Password that will be used to sign in to account

  ![Company Sign Up Page](/assets/screenshots/userDetailsSignup.png)

4. **Confirm Details**

   - Verify all information looks correct

5. **Verify Email**

- Check your inbox for a verification email\*
  **NOTE: Do not change your web browser when checking email**
  - Didn't recieve verification email? Click "Didn't Recieve Email" and enter the email used in sign up
- Click the verification link to activate the account and go to the login page

6. **Log In**

- Follow steps in [Login](http://localhost:3000/docs/auth/login) to complete logging in with your newly created account

### Inviting Team Members

To invite members to your company's account, go to [http://localhost:3000/settings/team](http://localhost:3000/settings/team)
